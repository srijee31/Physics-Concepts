# Scalars and Vectors

## What is it?

Physical quantities are classified into scalars and vectors based on whether direction is involved or not.

---

## Simple Meaning

👉 Scalar = only magnitude (value)  
👉 Vector = magnitude + direction  

---

## Example

Scalars:
- Distance  
- Speed  
- Mass  

Vectors:
- Displacement  
- Velocity  
- Force  

---

## Why it is used?

- To understand motion and forces  
- To describe physical quantities correctly  
- Important in mechanics and physics calculations  

---

## Key Points

- Scalars have only magnitude  
- Vectors have magnitude and direction  
- Vectors are represented using arrows  
- Direction is important in vector quantities
