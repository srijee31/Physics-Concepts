# Measurement and Errors

## What is it?

Measurement is the process of finding the value of a physical quantity using standard units.

Errors are the differences between the measured value and the true value.

---

## Simple Meaning

👉 Measurement = finding value  
👉 Error = small mistake in measurement  

---

## Example

- Measuring length with a scale  
- If actual length = 10 cm  
- Measured length = 9.8 cm  
👉 Error = 0.2 cm  

---

## Why it is used?

- To get accurate results  
- To understand limitations of measurement  
- To improve precision in experiments  

---

## Key Points

- Measurement uses standard units  
- Errors are always present in measurement  
- Errors can be minimized but not completely removed  
- Important for scientific accuracy
