# Fundamental and Derived Quantities

## What is it?

Physical quantities are divided into two types: fundamental quantities and derived quantities.

---

## Simple Meaning

👉 Fundamental = basic quantities  
👉 Derived = made from fundamental quantities  

---

## Example

Fundamental Quantities:
- Length (meter)
- Mass (kilogram)
- Time (second)

Derived Quantities:
- Speed = distance / time
- Force = mass × acceleration

---

## Why it is used?

- To classify physical quantities clearly  
- To understand how complex quantities are formed  
- To simplify calculations in physics  

---

## Key Points

- Fundamental quantities are basic and independent  
- Derived quantities depend on fundamental quantities  
- Derived quantities are formed using formulas  
- SI units are based on fundamental quantities
