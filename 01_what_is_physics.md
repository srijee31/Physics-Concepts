# What is Physics?

## What is it?

Physics is the branch of science that studies matter, energy, and how they interact with each other.

---

## Simple Meaning

👉 Physics explains how things work in the real world.

---

## Example

- Why objects fall to the ground  
- How a car moves  
- Why the sky appears blue  

---

## Why it is used?

- To understand natural phenomena  
- To develop technology (mobiles, machines, etc.)  
- To explain how the universe works  

---

## Key Points

- Physics studies matter and energy  
- It explains everyday phenomena  
- It is the foundation of many technologies  
- It helps us understand the universe
